1stDayHack 2020

1stDayHack is a week-long online Machine Learning (ML) hackathon designed for high school students in Malaysia. Students will learn not only to build new solution but to apply them on real world problems.


Held virtually. 5th to 13th December 2020.

Registration is open for all Malaysian High School Students


Learn. Build. Innovate.

Learn

Students will have a week long to learn from online resources and guidance from mentors with their fellow team members.

Build

Students get to build their own Machine Learning prototype and gather feedback from mentors during the 1 week long period before heading on to showcase their products in the final pitching competition.

Innovate

Students will also be exposed to cutting-edge technologies and shown how to create innovative solutions that addresses real-world problems with them.
