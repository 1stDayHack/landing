1stDayHack 2020

1stDayHack is a week-long online Machine Learning (ML) hackathon designed for high school students in Malaysia. Students will learn not only to build new solution but to apply them on real world problems.


Held virtually, from 9th December - 17th December 2020

Learn. Build. Innovate

Learn

Students will have a week long to learn from online resources and meet with mentors online.

Build

Students get to build their own Machine Learning prototype in the 1 week long period and gather feedback from mentors.

Innovate

Students are also encourage to brainstorm whether their solution is really helping to make the world a better place.
