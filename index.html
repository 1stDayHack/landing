What is 1stDayHack?

1stDayHack is one of the first hackathon in Malaysia that caters only to secondary high school students. We want to inspire, engage and give hands-on experience to the next generation of Malaysian youths in the cutting edge of modern computing technology - Machine Learning. 60 students from various schools, public and private, across 2 days to learn and develop softwares that are highly practical and technologically sophisticated, not merely a coding exercise. Included in the program are various talks, dedicated workshops and tutorials to provide sufficient foundation in topics such as fundamentals of programming and product design. In addition, a dedicated mentor will be assigned to each team to guide them throughout the entire experience.

Tentative Tracks

The theme for the hackathon will be Practical Machine Learning.

Online social media sentiment analysis with Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Highly competitive object classifier with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks

Automated web crawler for online products aggregation and analysis
